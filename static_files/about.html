Nethack-a-Day

Nethack, one day at a time.

Nethack is one of the best games of all time, and one of our favorites. Unfortunately, we die a lot.

A lot.

We wondered if a more stately pace would improve our gameplay. Why not try one action a day?

Also, since the action's going to roll out somewhat deliberately, how about a running narrative?

Welcome to Nethack-a-Day.
